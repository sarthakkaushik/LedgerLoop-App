# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a respectful, harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Focus on technical discussion, not personal attacks.
- Accept feedback and collaborate in good faith.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or abusive language
- Trolling, personal insults, or intimidation
- Publishing private information without consent

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove or edit comments, reject contributions, or block users for behavior that violates this code.

## Reporting

Report conduct issues privately to the maintainer through GitHub profile contact.
